Missionaries of Saint Teresa

 
This missionary insitute was founded in Santa Rosa de Osos, Antioquia, Colombia, by Bishop Miguel Angel Builes on april 11,  1929.

They are a missionary orden in Colombia and minister around the world, as the founder had intended. At the moment, this order has about 500 sisters.

The Center of this Institute is in Medellin, Anitoquia, Colombia.

In addition to being missionaries, the sisters work in schools, and attend to works of charity.

In Guadalupe, the sisters have been working as missionaries since the 20 of september of 1986.

The missionaries of Saint Teresa are in the following:

  2. Brazil
