Father Georg Nigsch

  • Diocesan priest of the Diocesis of Feldkirch, Vorarlberg, Austria.
  • Born on the 21st of May 1957 in Austria
  • Elementary school in Schoppernau, Austria
  • High school in Bregenz, Austria
  • University studies of philosophy and theology in Innsbruck, Austria, and Cromwell, CT, USA.
  • Ordained as Catholic priest in 1983 in Dornbirn, Austria
  • From 1983-1989 in the parish of St.Martin-Dornbirn, Austria
  • From 1989-1991 in the parish of St.Gebhard-Dornbirn, Austria
  • Since 1991, missionary in the "Vicariato Apostolico de Zamora", Ecuador.
