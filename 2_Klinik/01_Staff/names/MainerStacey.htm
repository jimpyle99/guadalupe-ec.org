Observations about the Medical Brigade November 2006

for more infomtion and many pictures click here

 

We had an interesting jornada in that the river was low and we had to make different plans. But it was a good trip! I am hoping that the joronadas can be organized differently so that on-going health care can be done 4 times a year. I would like to see patients with diabetes, hypertension and depression have written charts/notes so that providers can see how they are doing 4 times a year. And, I envision going back to the same area so that this can occur. It is my hope that the community develop short and long term goals so that we are truly meeting their needs. We have returned to Guayzimi twice now and it is very positive to see the community leaders be involved with these jornadas. In addition, I would like to see health education be a very strong component of these jornadas.
