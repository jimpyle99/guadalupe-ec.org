Room and Board in the "Residencia" of Guadalupe

 

 

For the volunteers of the clinic, we have nice and comfortable accomodatins right above the mission clinic.

 

Room and Board is for free for all volunteers.

 

The residence has 6 dormitories (4 doubles and 2 singles)

Each bedroom has its own bathroom with hot showers.

 

Furthermore, there is a nice living and dinning room, a well equipped kitchen, a laundry room with a washing machine and also a roomy balcony with a wonderful view of the jungle.

 

In our "Residencia", you will find a computer with an internet connection for free.

 

For your free time, you have many videos and DVDs. Also, you will find a nice library with many books to read.

 

For your walks, you will need most of the time rubber boots. You can pick any size you need.

 

For breakfast, everybody is on their own, but you can get all you need in the sister´s residence.

 

For lunch and dinner, you can eat in the sister´s residence together with the parish priest and the sisters. In case you don´t want to eat there occasionally, we ask you kindly advise the kitchen a day beforehand.
