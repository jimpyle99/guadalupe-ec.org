APPLICATION FORM FOR VOLUNTEERING IN OUR CLINIC

First of all, thank you very much for your interest in working with us. Please, be so kind and respond briefly to the following questions in one way or an other:  

  1. Please, write your name, address, profession, age, male or female?

  2. What is your motivation or intention for working as a volunteer?

  3. What expectations do you have in working in our health project (please check our website for more information:   www.guadalupe-ec.org )

  4. We would appreciate it if you could send us your CV (curriculum vitae) or some information about your professional history.

  5. In your country, are you legally accepted as a professional?
    (In case you come, we ask you kindly to send us by e-mail or fax your professional licence with the expiration date and also bring the same copy of your professional licence for our Province Health Department in Ecuador). 

  6. How long do you want to work with us?

  7. When could you start?

  8. Do you want to come alone or with husband/wife/children?

  9. Do you know Spanish?

  10. What do you think is the biggest challenge in working within an other culture?

  11. We accept volunteers of any religious denominations or beliefs. But how do you think about Christian/Catholic values?

  12. How did you hear about us?

Please send this to the following e-mail: lindo@guadalupe-ec.org
