This modern facility is located on the grounds of a mission in the town of Guadalupe. In addition to examining rooms, there is an operating theater, dental suites, optometric services, an extensive pharmacy, and a rudimentary laboratory. They have also organized mobile health fairs in other remote underserved impoverished villages of the Oriente, the Ecuadorian Amazon. Volunteers have come from the United States, Great Britain, Switzerland, Canada, Austria, the Netherlands, Germany, and Ireland. There have been internists, pediatricians, general practitioners, otolaryngologists, ophthalmologists, emergency physicians, dentists, and a general surgeon. Nurse practitioners and physician assistants have attended. A German medical student and a nurse practitioner student from The Yale School of Nursing have rotated through this venue. Volunteers of all faiths have come for two weeks or for several months.
