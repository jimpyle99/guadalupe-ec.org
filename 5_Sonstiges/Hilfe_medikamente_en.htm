To Whom It May Concern,

Thank you for your interest in our Clinic.  We have grown over the past several years, and more and more patients are coming to depend on us to provide health care for the poor communities in our region of Zamora-Chinchipe in Ecuador.   Whether you come as a volunteer, or support us from home, we appreciate all donations; in fact, we could not survive without your help.

4.        Antimiotics (Topical, Oral and Vaginal)

5.        Aspirin

6.        Antihypertensives

7.        Antinflammatories, Pain Medications

8.        Antacids and Ulcer medications

9.        Steroid Creams

10.     Beta agonist inhalers (Albuterol, Serevent)

11.     Antibiotics syrups and tablets (Azithromycin, Doxycycline, Ciprofloxacin would be especially helpful) 

12.     Sensor discs for Bayer Glucometer Style 3952H or visual color-change blood glucose test strips   

13.     Topical antibiotic creams
