VERY IMPORTANT MESSAGE FOR SENDING PACKAGES
Please contact P. Jorge Nigsch before sending ANY packages to Guadalupe. Packages cannot weigh more than 4 kg (8.8 lbs). The Ecuadorian government has new Custom rules which makes it very difficult to import anything.

WICHTIGE MITTEILUNG BEZÜGLICH PAKETE
