Bus

 Vom Bus-Hauptterminal südlich der Altstadt in Quito; verschiedene Busgesellschaften (LOJA; VIAJEROS; SANTA; PANAMERICANA..), nicht schlecht sind Nachfahrten; es gibt die Fahrt über das Hochland (Sierra) oder über die Küste. Nachts ist die Küstenroute nicht anzuraten; Kosten ca. 10 Dollar; nehmen Sie nicht den billigsten Bus; für einen angenehmeren Bus zahlt man gerne ein bißchen mehr. Fahrtzeit von Quito nach Loja ca. 14 bis 18 Stunden.

Von Loja nach Guadalupe

Bus

 Vom Busterimal in Loja fahren zwei Direktbusse bis nach Guadalupe und von dort weiter bis nach Yacuambi oder 28 de Mayo.

Abfahrtszeit um ca. 1 Uhr mittags. Bus-Firma: CARIAMANGA. Fahrpreis minimal; der zweite Direktbus fährt um ca. 2 Uhr früh von Loja weg und kommt um 6 Uhr früh in Guadalupe an.

Der Bus hält überall auf der Straße, wo Leute ein- und aussteigen wollen. Fahrtzeit von Loja bis nach Guadalupe 3 bis 4 Stunden.

Wenn Sie keinen Direktbus in Loja erwischen, steigen Sie in irgendeinen Bus ein, der in den Oriente, d.h. nach Zamora fährt. Im Busterminal in Zamora können Sie in einen kleinen Bus umsteigen , der Richtung Guadalupe fährt.

Von Loja nach Zamora sind des ca. 60 km., von Zamora bis Guadalupe ca. 30 km.

Mit dem Privatauto sind es von Loja bis nach Guadalupe ca. zweieinhalb Stunden Fahrzeit.

 

 

 

BY BUS:

With the "Trole" (those are the electric buses north - south) or with taxi you will reach the main bus terminal south of the old part of the city of Quito.

There are direct buses from Quito to Loja;
Running time approx. 16 to 20 hours.
Do not take the cheapest bus; for a more pleasant bus one pays gladly a little more. The best you travel at night.
Departure in Quito is approx. 6 or 7 o'clock in the evening and you arrive the next day in the morning in Loja.

BY AIRPLANE:

There are two airlines: TAME and ICARO. Tame flies e.g. each day except Sunday. The flight with TAME costs - a way - approx. 40 dollar; Flying time 50 minutes. ICARO is somewhat more expensive. Takeoff in Quito always at 6 o'clock early. It is advisable to be not later like 5:00 early in national airport building. To buy the ticket best or mehrer days beforehand in one of the TAME offices. In the national airport building in Quito gives it also an TAME office. Arrival in Loja: 6: 50 a.m. The airport is not in Loja, but in CATAMAYO or LA TOMA. From the airport in Catamayo until Loja gives it a very good taxi connection. Travel time approx. 45 minutes. Price of transportation for taxi: 2. - Dollar per seat carrier top

 

THE WAY FROM LOJA TO GUADALUPE: Look on the map Loja and far east the province capital up Zamora. Guadalupe is situated northeast from Zamora in a northern side valley of the Zamora Haupttales, in the Yacuambi valley. BY BUS: From the Busterimal in Loja two direct penalty drive until Guadalupe and from there far until Yacuambi or 28 de Mayo. Departure time around approx. 1 o'clock at noon. Bus company: CARIAMANGA. Price of transportation:. The second direct bus drives around approx. 2 o'clock early from Loja away and arrives at 6 o'clock early at Guadalupe. The bus holds everywhere on the road, where people want to in and step out. Running time of Loja until Guadalupe 3 to 4 hours. If you do not get a direct bus in Loja, enter any bus, which drives into the Oriente, i.e. to Zamora. In the bus terminal in Zamora you can transfer into a small bus, the direction Guadalupe drive. From Loja to Zamora approx. 60 km are, from Zamora to Guadalupe approx. 30 km. With the private car there is of Loja until Guadalupe approx. two and a half hours travel time.
